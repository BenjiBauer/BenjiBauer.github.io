DLRG

Trailer and Re-Design




In another project at the university I was shooting a general image trailer for the „Jugend DLRG“ (Youth German Life Saving Association) with two other students. This was a part of one elektive of my study. My main tasks were especially filming, editing (hence the sound and image postprocessing) and the music, which I produced with a friend.

Furthermore we also designed a number of redesign drafts for their website www.dlrg-jugend.de. Difficult was that the DLRG wanted to keep all hyperlinks on their position and did not want to summarize them in a submenu.
One of my drafts, you see below, they liked best. Unfortunately it was not possible to realize it, because of a number of reasons.
