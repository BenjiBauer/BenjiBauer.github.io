About my study path

A lot of people never heard about my study path „Interactive Media Design“. It is about how we design our future and, for instance improve our everyday lives through interactive things. This ranges from a simple website or app up to the „Internet of Things“ and „Ambient Intelligence“.
It consists of a mixture of computer sciences / technology, design, management, linked to strategy, it offers a great insight into the world of interactive media.
Besides the lectures we work in small groups and create a project on a given topic each semester. First we start with an idea, do research about it, build a detailed and well elaborated concept and develop a prototype for it. Teamwork is essential for success. Along with some large public presentations we do small presentations about our intermediate state every week, where we get feedback from the professors and also from the other students.
The lessons and teaching are in german as well as in english.
